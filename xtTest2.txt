\s1 A \p \k Abba\k*: Aramaic word that means <<the father.>> It is an especially intimate term (\xt Mar 14:36; Rom 8:15; Gal 4:6). 
\p \k Abib\k*: Early name for the first month of the Jewish calendar (\xt Exo 13:4; 23:15; 34:18; Deu 16:1\xt*). After the Babylonian captivity this month was called Nisan (\xt Neh 2:1; Est 3:7).
